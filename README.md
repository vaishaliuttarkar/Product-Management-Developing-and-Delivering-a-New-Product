**#Objectives**:

To develop a comprehensive product management strategy for the Mobile WB-3000 across its lifecycle, testing, launch, and retirement.  

**#Key Deliverables**:

Beta Testing Plan: Plan for testing the product with users and collecting feedback before launch.

Demand Generation Plan: Strategy for marketing, promotions, and generating customer interest.

Product Launch Plan: Roadmap for successfully introducing the product to the market.

End-of-Life (EOL) Plan: Strategy for product retirement and customer transition to updated products.
